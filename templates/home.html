Twitter Sentiment

Questions and Interest

The goal of this project was to find out more about Twitter and utilize artificial intelligence, specifically Natural Language Processing (NLP) to assertain the general sentiment of tweets on various days of the week and whether the words are positive or negative. Because of the potential size of the data we opted to select three days that included a Monday (March), a Friday (April), and April Fools Day. In order find out the general sentiment we asked the three following questions:

1. What are the variations of sentiment among days of the week, Monday versus Friday? Are Mondays more negative and are Fridays more positive?

2. What are the top tweets tweeted on Mondays? What hashtags tweeted on Monday's?

3. What are the top hashtags for the selected days?
